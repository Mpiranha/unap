About The Award

The African pillars award is a yearly accolade, designed to thank and celebrate Africa’s most impactful Personalities from different walks of life; men and women who have made positive statements with their lives and as well made significant contributions with their resources to foster hope and bolster speedy development in their respective countries and regions, in accordance with the 17 SDGS of the United Nations.

All nominees for this award are selected by local and international SDG ambassadors and observers. Based on evident works and public recommendations.
